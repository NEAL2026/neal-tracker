Section 1
What is the Commission Tracker?
📢
The short version Any time you upsell a product or service to a client — a water treatment, aeration system, lighting, fish, plants, anything — you log it here and earn a commission on top of your regular pay. The more you log, the more you earn.
% Commission Percentage of the sale
You earn a set percentage of whatever the sale price is. Sell a $200 treatment system at 10% → you earn $20.
Flat Rate Fixed dollar amount
Some products pay a fixed amount regardless of price. Sell a Koi package → you earn $30 flat.
🏆
LeaderboardYour commissions appear on the team leaderboard. Top earners get recognized. This is real money — don't leave it on the table by forgetting to log.

Section 4 — The Most Important Part
How to Log an Upsell
⭐
Log it the same dayLog your upsells while you're still at the job or on your way home. Don't wait — you'll forget the details and that's money lost.
1
Tap "Log Upsell" tab
It's the first tab when you log in. You'll see the upsell form below it.
2
Enter the Date
It defaults to today — just confirm it's the right date.
3
Select the Product
Choose from the dropdown — all the products Sean has set up with commission rates are listed here.
⚡ Quick Log: If you sold the same thing recently, it appears at the top as a Quick Log button. One tap pre-fills everything.
4
Enter Customer Name & Sale Price
Type the client's name or property address. Then enter the sale price — the commission calculates automatically so you can see exactly what you're earning before you submit.
5
Add Invoice Number
Enter the Jobber invoice number so everything ties together.
6
Tap "Log Upsell"
Done. Your commission is recorded and shows up in My Upsells immediately.
💡
What counts as an upsell?Anything you recommend and the client says yes to — water treatments, aeration, lighting, fish, plants, auto dosers, filters, anything on the product list. If you mention it and they buy it, log it.
📊
Section 5
Checking Your Commissions
📋
My Upsells tab — shows every upsell you've logged this month, the sale price, commission earned, and the status (pending → approved → paid).
📈
Your dashboard — shows your total commissions, how you're tracking against your goal, and a 6-month history chart.
🏆
Leaderboard — tap the leaderboard button at the top of the screen to see where you stand against the team this month.
✅
Status meanings Pending — logged but not yet reviewed by manager.
Approved — confirmed and will be in your next payout.
Denied — something was off, check with Sean.
📤
Section 6
Submitting Your Month
📅
Do this at the end of every monthBefore the month closes out, you need to officially submit your upsells for that month. This tells management your list is complete and ready for review and payment.
1
Tap "Submit Month" tab
You'll see a summary of all your upsells for the current month and the total commission amount.
2
Review the List
Make sure everything is there. If something is missing, go back to Log Upsell and add it before submitting.
3
Tap "Submit Month"
Confirm when prompted. Your submission goes to Sean and Kristina for review. Once approved, your commission gets processed for payout.
⚠️ Once submitted you can't add more upsells to that month. Make sure you've logged everything first.
🚨
Don't forget to submitIf you don't hit Submit Month, your upsells won't be processed for payment. Logging them is only half — submitting closes it out.
