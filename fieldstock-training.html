Why We're Doing This
🚐
Every van has its own inventory. At the start of the season, Sean loads your vehicle with the materials assigned to it. That's your stock to work with.
📋

One new responsibility. When you use something off your van, you need to restock it through FieldStockPro before the end of your day. That's it.

Section 3
Restocking Your Van
ℹ️
When to restockAny time you grab something off your van for a job — whether it's a bottle of treatment, a part, or supplies — you need to log the restock through FieldStockPro when you return to the shop. Do it before you leave for the day.
1
Open FieldStockPro
Log in at fieldstockpro.com or open it from your phone.
2
Go to Products
Tap Products in the left menu. Search for the item you used by name, or use the built-in barcode scanner.
📷 Tip: Use the barcode scanner — it's the fastest way. Look for the small scanner icon right next to the search bar inside the app. Tap it and point your phone camera at the barcode on the product box — it finds the item instantly.
3
Select the Item
Tap on the product to open it. You'll see current stock levels across all vehicles and locations.
4
Add Stock to Your Van
Select your vehicle (Red Truck, Blue Van, Yellow Van, etc.), enter the quantity you're putting back, and confirm.
🚐 Make sure you select YOUR vehicle — not another crew's van.
5
Done
Your van inventory is updated. Sean can see the stock moved from the shop to your vehicle and everything balances.
🚨
Don't forgetIf you use something and don't restock it in the system, your van shows more stock than you actually have. This causes ordering problems and makes it look like product is missing. Please do it the same day.
🔍
Section 4
Checking What's Been Issued to Your Job

When Sean issues materials for a specific job, here's how you know what's yours to use.

📄
Check the physical printout firstWhen Sean issues items to a job he generates and prints a list of everything that's been assigned. He'll either leave it with the materials at the shop or hand it directly to the team lead before the job. This printout is your primary reference — check it before you start.
📱
Don't have the printout? Check FieldStockProIf you don't have a physical copy, open FieldStockPro → tap Jobs → find your job → review the issued materials list. Everything Sean assigned will be there.
1
Check the Physical Printout
Sean leaves this with the materials or gives it to the team lead. This is your list of what's been issued for the job.
2
Use Only What's on the List
These items are already accounted for. Do NOT add them to your Jobber invoice — they have already been issued to the job.
✅ These items are paid for and tracked. Invoicing them in Jobber would double-count them.
3
Need Something Not on the List?
Text or call Sean before pulling anything extra from your van. He'll either add it to the issued list or tell you to put it on the Jobber invoice.
💬
When in doubt — ask firstNever guess whether something should be invoiced or was already issued. A quick text saves a lot of headaches on both ends.
✅
Section 5
The Rules — Simple Version
✅
DO: Restock your van in FieldStockPro any time you use something — same day.
✅
DO: Check the physical printout (or FieldStockPro) before a job when Sean has issued materials.
✅
DO: Invoice materials in Jobber the same way you always have for standard jobs.
✅
DO: Speak up if something in the system isn't working right — Sean wants to know.
❌
DON'T: Invoice items in Jobber that Sean already issued to the job in FieldStockPro — that double-counts them.
❌
DON'T: Use stock off your van without logging the restock in FieldStockPro.
❌
DON'T: Move stock between vans or locations on your own — that's Sean's job.
