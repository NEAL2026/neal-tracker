➕ How to Log an Upsell
⭐
Log it the same dayWhile at the job or on the way home. Don't wait — you'll forget and that's money lost.
1
Tap "Log Upsell" tab — first tab after login
2
Confirm the date (defaults to today)
3
Select the product from the dropdown — commission calculates automatically
⚡ Quick Log: Recent products appear at the top — one tap pre-fills everything
4
Enter customer name or address + sale price
5
Add Jobber invoice # (optional but helpful)
6
Tap "Log Upsell" — done, shows up immediately
