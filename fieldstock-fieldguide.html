📦 Restocking Your Van
1
Open fieldstockpro.com on your phone
2
Tap Products → search by name or tap the barcode scanner icon next to the search bar → point camera at the box
📷 Scanner is the fastest — look for the icon right next to the search bar
3
Tap on the product to open it
4
Select your vehicle, enter the quantity, confirm
🚐 Double-check you selected YOUR van — not another crew's
🚨
Do it the same dayIf you don't log it, your van shows more stock than you have — causes ordering problems and looks like missing product.
